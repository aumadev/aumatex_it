What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device to provide or improve a service. This site uses them in compliance with GDPR, the Italian Data Protection Authority's guidelines and the ePrivacy rules.

Cookies we use

  • Navigation and session cookies to deliver pages, maintain language preferences and ensure basic functionality.
  • Security and load-balancing cookies issued by the hosting provider (Aruba) to protect the infrastructure and distribute traffic.
  • A consent cookie that stores your choices from the banner for up to 12 months.
  • No analytics, marketing or profiling cookies are enabled on this site.

Profiling and third-party cookies

We do not use third-party marketing platforms, pixels or advertising networks. The only third party involved is Aruba Business as hosting provider, limited to technical cookies.

Consent management

Essential cookies are always active because they are necessary for the site to operate. The banner lets you accept or reject optional categories; since no optional cookies are active, your choice mainly concerns the storage of consent itself.

Change your choices

You can reopen the cookie banner at any time from the footer link "Cookie preferences" and update your consent.

Manage cookies via your browser

You can delete or block cookies from your browser settings (e.g., Safari, Chrome, Edge, Firefox). Blocking technical cookies may prevent the site from working correctly.

Rights and contacts

To exercise your privacy rights or obtain more information, contact info@aumatex.it or see the Privacy Policy.
