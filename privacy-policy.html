Privacy Policy

This notice explains how Aumatex srls processes personal data in compliance with GDPR (EU) 2016/679 and applicable Italian law.

Data controller

Aumatex srls · Via Umberto Biancamano, 25 · 00185 Roma (RM) · info@aumatex.it · aumatex@pec.it · +39 351 8860030.

Legal references

Processing is based on Articles 6(1)(b), 6(1)(c) and 6(1)(f) GDPR for service delivery, legal compliance and legitimate interest in security and site operation.

Data processed

  • Technical navigation data (IP address, device/browser info, timestamps) generated by access logs for security and performance.
  • Contact details and message content you provide (name, email, phone, company, requested service) when you reach us.
  • Cookie preferences saved locally and technical cookies issued by the hosting provider (Aruba Business) necessary to serve the site.

Purposes and legal bases

  • Allow browsing of the site and deliver requested pages and assets.
  • Respond to information, commercial or support requests you submit.
  • Protect the platform, detect anomalies or abuse, and maintain service continuity.
  • Fulfil legal obligations and manage any defensive needs.

Retention

Technical logs are kept only for the time needed to ensure security and diagnostics (normally up to 12 months). Contact requests are retained for the time strictly necessary to handle the conversation and up to 24 months for traceability. Cookie consent choices are stored for up to 12 months.

Hosting and third parties

The site is hosted on Aruba Business (Linux) within the EU. Aruba may set only technical cookies for load balancing and security. No analytics, marketing or profiling cookies are enabled and data are not shared for advertising purposes.

Data transfers

Data are stored in the EU. If future transfers outside the EU/EEA become necessary, they will follow adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses in line with GDPR.

Your rights

  • Access to your personal data and information about processing.
  • Rectification of inaccurate data and completion of incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") within the limits of law.
  • Restriction of processing in the cases provided for by Article 18 GDPR.
  • Portability of data you provided, in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection to processing based on legitimate interest, including security monitoring.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).

How to exercise your rights

You can write to info@aumatex.it or aumatex@pec.it, or call +39 351 8860030. We will respond as soon as possible and, in any case, within the terms set by GDPR.

Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures proportionate to the risks (TLS encryption, access control, least-privilege accounts, monitored backups) to safeguard data.

Updates

We may update this privacy notice to reflect legal or technical changes. Last update: 11/03/2026.
